FAQ

Why qeeply?

Most small businesses have similar bookkeeping problem - unresponsive bookkeeper, expensive fees, and stack of unprocessed receipts. qeeply is born to solve these problems. Our goal is to provide accurate and timely monthly financial report so you can make important decision and ultimately growing your business.

Who is qeeply?

We are a team of experienced bookkeepers and technology geeks who try to simplify this monthly time consuming process and make our customers lives easy.

What is qeeply's pricing model?

We are working hard to determine our pricing. We are confident that our pricing will be competitive (in the range of $125 CAD and above).

How does qeeply work?

You will have a dedicated bookkeeper assigned to you and to answer any of your questions. Your monthly process is simple:

  • You upload bank, credit card statements, and any other information relevant to your books such as expenses to be reimbursed, and supplier/customer invoices
  • Our bookkeeper will process the statements including allocating appropriate sales tax and collaborate with you to obtain accurate information for allocating transactions
  • Monthly financial statement that can be shared with your accountant, employees, or business partners, will be posted at the latest 20 days after month-end

Does qeeply work with my accountant?

When it comes time to do your taxes, we will work directly with your accountant to ensure all tax filings are completed in a timely manner and provide them with the necessary information.

Does qeeply offer invoicing and payroll services?

Unfortunately, qeeply does not provide invoicing, bill tracking, payroll, or inventory management. However, our experienced bookkeeper will be able to assist you in automating these processes.

What accounting method does qeeply use?

qeeply does bookkeeping on a cash basis (we record transactions based on your bank statement transactions). This method is the easiest and simplest to help you manage your small business. However, we can also do accrual basis; please consult with one of our experienced bookkeepers.
